How to Use our Crop Suggestion Tool

Live Weather Update

"Using Weather Data for Smarter Farming"

  • Access real-time weather updates for your specific location.
  • Monitor temperature, humidity, rainfall, and wind conditions.
  • Receive alerts for extreme weather events that may impact your crops.
  • Plan your farming activities based on accurate weather forecasts.

Instant Crop Suggestion

"Chooosing the Right Crop For Your Soil and Season"

  • Enter your soil type and current season correctly for best results
  • Follow Suggested crops as they are suited to your soil Ph and moisture
  • Rotate crops each season to maintain soil fertility

Crop Yield Predictor

"Estimating Your Harvest with Data-Driven Insights"

  • enter the crop name (eg., Rice,Wheat,Maize)
  • Enter your field area in acres
  • Provide the base yield (average kg per acre under normal conditions)
  • Enter the Rainfall factor (0.5-1.5)

    • 0.5–0.9 → Low rainfall

    1.0 → Average rainfall

    1.1–1.5 → High rainfall

  • Enter the Soil Fertility factor (0.5-1.5)

    • 0.5–0.9 → Poor fertility

    1.0 → Average fertility

    1.1–1.5 → High fertility

  • Enter the Fertilizer Efficiency factor (0.5-1.5)

    • 0.5–0.9 → Low efficiency

    1.0 → Average efficiency

    1.1–1.5 → High efficiency

  • Click on Predict Yield to view results
  • The formula used: Predicted Yield = Base Yield × Rainfall × Soil × Fertilizer × Area
